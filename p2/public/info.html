GALA PORRAS - KIM
UNSEEN FORCE
NOV 1, 2025 – DEC 5, 2025

Gala Porras-Kim is a Colombian-Korean-American contemporary interdisciplinary artist who lives and works in Los Angeles and London. She makes work about the social and political contexts that influence how intangible things, such as sounds, language and history, have been framed through the fields of linguistics, history and conservation.

She explores how contemporary institutions influence artifacts from the past, examining the temporal relationships that connect the present and the past.

The exhibition Unseen Force explores the influence of invisible forces and the effects of time on objects. It features the works of Gala Porras-Kim, who investigates artifact preservation and the social and historical contexts surrounding them, including connections between past objects and contemporary conservation methods. The exhibition emphasizes visual pieces shaped by environmental conditions rather than human intervention over time.
