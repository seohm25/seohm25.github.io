Why do we drawn to retro?

This website looks into the aesthetics of retro design. It started from my personal affection for the nostalgic feeling of retro and a question about why it continues to attract us. As people today return to film cameras and camcorders, the retro mood seems to hold a special place once again.

Through presenting works that I enjoy and that reflect this sensibility, I would like to explore what retro means from my own point of view..
